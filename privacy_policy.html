Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority

Version 1 (Dec 31 2024)

This document outlines how Focus Group, and affiliates (hereon referred to as “FG”), handles your data, be it personal or other information. As far as possible, we’ve used plain English to explain how we respect your privacy and why you should trust us with your data.

This Privacy Policy applies to all products, services and websites provided by FG (collectively referred to as the “FG Service”), except where otherwise noted. If any services have supplementary privacy statements, we’ll explain in more detail our specific privacy practices in relation to these services.

The FG Service is provided to two distinct groups. Firstly, our Service provides a platform and set of tools that enables people, companies and organizations (“app creators”) to create data-driven business apps for mobile and desktop devices. These apps are delivered to the second group, who interact with, and submit data through, the provided apps (“app users”). FG receives and handles information differently for app creators and app users, so we have split this Privacy Policy into two dedicated sections, one for each group.

SECTION 1: PRIVACY FOR APP CREATORS

Our approach to your data

  • Your data is yours. FG regards all content that you create within your account on the FG Service (forms, data sources, tasks, user accounts, email addresses etc.) as being your private property, owned wholly by you. FG does not sell your content or data to anyone, and we only process and make your data available as needed to provide the FG Service, with the exception of a limited set of circumstances (e.g. FG is compelled by a subpoena, or if you’ve given us permission to do so).
  • Your data hosting is based on your specified country at account signup. The FG Platform is hosted on Microsoft’s Azure cloud platform in 3 separate regions (“nodes”) across the world – specifically USA, West Europe and Australia. Customers that identify themselves as being located within Europe, Middle East and Africa are hosted within our West Europe (Amsterdam & Dublin) data centers. Similarly North and South American customers are hosted within our USA data centers, while East Asia and Oceania customers are hosted in our Australia East data centers. Once your account is provisioned on one of these nodes, there is no transfer or cross-population of your data to other nodes. So if, for example, your account is created on our West Europe node, then that is the only place your data will ever be hosted by us.
  • FG is committed to processing and storing your data securely.

Information that FG collects

FG collects information relating to you and your use of the FG Service from a variety of sources which are listed below, along with details of how we use this information.

Information we collect directly from you

  • Registration information. You need to create an FG account before you can build apps on the FG Service. Our account signup collects your name, email address, password, phone number, company name, country and time zone.
  • Billing information. If you make a payment to FG, we require that you provide your billing details, including name, address, email address and financial information corresponding to your selected method of payment (e.g. a credit card number and expiration date). If you provide a billing address, we will regard that as the location of the account holder. FG does not store or log any sensitive cardholder data provided by you or your app users. We use a PCI Level 1 compliant credit card processor which securely stores all card details and processes card payments.
  • Account settings. You can set various preferences and personal details within the FG Service. For example, your default language, time zone and other meta-data values can be specified within the Edit User and Organisation Setup pages of our backend web portal.
  • App data. We store the data you and your app users upload, import and otherwise submit to us.
  • Other data you intentionally share. We may store or collect your personal information or data if you explicitly send it to us for specified purposes. For example, if you give us a customer testimonial or participate in an FG sponsored marketing event.
  • We don’t share, sell or use your app users’ information. FG will not contact your app users via email or other means unless you specifically request us to do so. We will not sell or make available your app users’ contact or other information to any third parties, aside from what is necessary to provide the FG Service (e.g. emails that you’ve configured are sent through our email delivery provider).

Information we collect indirectly or passively

  • Usage data. Like most software services today, FG collects usage data about you whenever you interact with our services. This can include which FG areas/pages you visit, what you click on, when you performed those actions, and so on. Additionally our servers may keep short-term log files that record data each time a device accesses the FG Service. These log files contain data about the nature of each access, including originating IP addresses, internet service providers, the files viewed on our site (e.g., HTML pages, graphics, etc.), operating system versions, and timestamps.
  • Device data. FG collects data from the device you use to access our services, such as your IP address, operating system version, device manufacturer and model, and browser type. We may also infer your geographic location based on your IP address.
  • Referral data. If you arrive at an FG website from an external source (such as a link on another website or in an email), FG records information about the source that referred you to us.
  • Information from third parties. FG may collect your personal information or data from third parties, if you have given permission to those third parties to share your information.
  • Information from page tags. FG uses third-party tracking services that employ cookies and page tags to collect aggregated and anonymized data about visitors to our websites. This data includes usage and user statistics. Emails sent by FG may include page tags that allow us to collect information about who opened those emails and clicked on links in them. We do this to measure the performance of our email messaging and to learn how to improve email deliverability and open rates.

To FG, your app data is private and owned by you. FG does not use your app data other than as described in this Privacy Policy unless we have your written consent.   • App user account deleted by an account administrator. Your nominated administrator users can delete app user accounts at any time. This disables the target user account immediately, but the user and associated data may be retained by FG for up to 30 days prior to being deleted. Depending on when you request it, restoration of the user account may be possible by our support team.
